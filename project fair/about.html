Who we are?

At Ristrett'o, we believe in more than just
serving food — we create unforgettable dining experiences.
Located in the heart of the city, we pride
ourselves on offering a diverse menu crafted
from the freshest, locally sourced ingredients.
Whether you’re joining us for a cozy dinner
with family or celebrating a special occasion,
our warm and welcoming atmosphere will
make you feel right at home.

Our cozy, modern decor with ocean-inspired
touches makes it the perfect spot for a romantic
evening, a family gathering, or a casual meal with
friends. We pride ourselves on our friendly staff,
who are always ready to provide recommendations
and ensure you have the best experience possible.
Join us for breakfast, lunch, or dinner, and don’t
miss out on our signature cocktails and extensive
wine selection. Whether you're a local or visiting
from afar, we look forward to welcoming you with
open arms and a table full of delicious food!

Our team
